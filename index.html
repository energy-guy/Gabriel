The "Fixed Protection" Plan

Lock in a single rate for your entire contract. Ideal for businesses that need strict budget consistency.

The Positives
  • 100% Budget certainty
  • Protection from market spikes
  • "Set it and forget it"
The Negatives
  • Higher "risk premium" built-in
  • Miss out if market prices drop

The "Market Savings" Plan

Pay what the market actually costs. Ideal for high-usage businesses that can be flexible with operations.

The Positives
  • Lowest overall average cost
  • Direct access to market drops
  • No markup for price insurance
The Negatives
  • Variable monthly bills
  • Vulnerable to extreme weather
